We were tasked to create a interactive vignette about a part of our lives using the p5.js framework. My interaction is about 3 things I do as part of my morning routine before I leave home.

The first scene is meant to depict me pressing the snooze button on my phone. The instruction for the controls for the first scene is shown as a pop-up, which requires the pressing of the Spacebar to dismiss. From there, the “minigame” starts. The minigame uses the WASD control on the keyboard. The viewer is to interact by moving the hand over to the snooze button to extend the timer in the centre of the scene. Eventually, you will have to wake up and that lead into the next scene.

The second scene is about me trying to make my morning cup of coffee. The ingredients to make the coffee will move past the screen. The user uses the A, S, D letter on the keyboard to slow down, pour and speed up the movement of the ingredient. The user can choose to follow the recipe on the wall or just go with their instinct.

The third scene is about my last routine check around the apartment to check and switch off any power switches and light switches. Letters will pop out from random parts of the scene, and the user is to press the corresponding keys on the keyboard and gradually turn off all the lights.
