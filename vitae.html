Vitae (2018)

(Latin)Life

The object was printed using PolyJet technology and make use of its capability to print multiple materials with different hardness. My idea was based on organic life forms where the soft, flexible flesh covers the hard bone-like structure with organic-looking holes.

The project was part of the course work of DSDN104: Digital Creation for the Bachelor of Design Innovation (BDI) at Victoria University of Wellington (VUW).
