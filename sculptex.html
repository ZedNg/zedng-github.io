Sculpting Assets (2019)

Part of the university course work allows me to create random models and assets to practice 3D sculpting. I had created a Bonsai, a monocycle and a sci-fi looking metallic plant. The models were sculpted and rendered on 3D-Coat.

The project was part of the course work of MDDN241: 3D Modelling and Animation II for the Bachelor of Design Innovation (BDI) at Victoria University of Wellington (VUW).
