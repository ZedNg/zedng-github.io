Letting Go...Again (2020)
2D Animation
Letting Go...Again (2020)

via GIPHY

We were tasked to create a short loopable 2D animation using Adobe Animate and drawing frame by frame.

I depicted a scene where a hand is holding onto a rope so tightly that it started bleeding. Only when the hand decide to let go, the blood droplet "morphed" into a balloon that flew away. The sequence loops back to the hand grabbing the rope again. I wanted to convey the message of how people have to keep learning to let go of something that is causing them a lot of pain.

Since I am doing this frame by frame, I took the opportunity to include texture to accentuate the drawn-on aesthetic. The animation of the hand is accomplished by rotoscoping shot footage of my hand doing the same action.

The project was part of the course work of ANFX 221: Digital 2D Animation I / Pakiwaituhi Matihiko Ahurua I for the Bachelor of Design Innovation (BDI) at Victoria University of Wellington (VUW).
