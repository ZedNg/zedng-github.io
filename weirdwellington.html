Weird Wellington (2018)
Click on image to view full size.

The project brief required us to use only primitive forms, and inspiration from buildings and landmarks of Wellington to create a narrative scene to tell a story. My story was about a dystopian Wellington after a great earthquake that spilt the land with magma flowing at the break. The area separated into a wealthy half and poverty-stricken half. I also got inspiration from “Batman: The Animated Series” and worked in an Art Deco style to the visual language. The scene was modelled and rendered in Autodesk Maya and Arnold Renderer.

The project was part of the course work of DSDN132: 3D Modelling & Animation I for the Bachelor of Design Innovation (BDI) at Victoria University of Wellington (VUW).
