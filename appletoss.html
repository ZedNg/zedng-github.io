Apple Tosser (2020)

My time at Victoria University of Wellington has allowed me to experiment with Virtual Reality using the Oculus Quest VR headset and Unreal Engine to create a simple interactive environment like this Apple Tosser experience.

The creation of this experience is aided by the VR Expansion Plugin and assets from Sketchfab and the UE Marketplace. This allowed me to quickly kitbash the environment and gave me more time to work on the interaction points using the Blueprint visual scripting system in Unreal Engine.

The concept of the experience was simple. The player stands at the cliff of a short hill and toss apples in any direction they wish. The apple will be regenerated back into the basket which allows the player to keep tossing apples infinitely.

The project was part of the course work of MDDN 222: Virtual Reality Studio / Taupuni Ao Marikofor the Bachelor of Design Innovation (BDI) at Victoria University of Wellington (VUW).
