The Creature (2019)
Click on image to view full size.

The brief was to create a character with multiple limbs and locomotor movement. It was inspired by a spider and a crab and was made to look a severed arm. The character was modelled with 3D-Coat and rendered in Autodesk Maya and Arnold Renderer.

The project was part of the course work of MDDN241: 3D Modelling and Animation II for the Bachelor of Design Innovation (BDI) at Victoria University of Wellington (VUW).
