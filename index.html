About Me

Originating from Singapore, Zi Yong Ng, known to others as Zed, is a multimedia designer currently based in Wellington, New Zealand.

Zed’s area of interest focuses on the integration of art, design, science and technology such as multimedia installations and digitally created designs.

Zed recently completed his degree for the Bachelor of Design Innovation majoring in Media Design at Victoria University of Wellington - School of Design Innovation.
